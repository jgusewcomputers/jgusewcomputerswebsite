About

I'm Jehiah.

I've worked in IT for most of my career — supporting systems, solving problems, and explaining things to people who just want their technology to work without the jargon. JGusew Computers is the natural extension of that: calm, practical help for everyday people and small businesses who need a clear next step, not a sales pitch.

I'm based in Sunbury, VIC. Everything comes through email — no phone queues, no on-site call-outs at this stage. You describe what you're dealing with in plain English, and I come back with questions, options, and a clear recommendation. If I can't help, I'll tell you who can.

A lot of technology advice pushes people toward buying something new, signing up for something, or handing over access they shouldn't hand over. I don't do that. The goal is always the simplest, safest next step — even if that step is "don't do anything yet."

I'm also careful about AI. It can be genuinely useful when the workflow is understood, but it should not be treated as a shortcut around judgement, privacy, or common sense.

Understand the problem before automating it.

The aim is not to add AI everywhere. The aim is to find the simplest useful system that keeps people safer, clearer, and less buried in admin.

The service is currently free. I'm using this period to refine how it works, gather feedback, and make sure the process is genuinely useful before charging for it. No obligation, no marketing list, no upsell.

If you have a technology problem you're not sure what to do about — a slow computer, a confusing email account, a scam you're worried about, a purchase you're unsure of — send it through. I'll have a look.
